'This dataset contains detailed information about customers of a telecom company, including demographics, service subscriptions, billing information, and churn status.
It captures key aspects such as whether a customer has phone or internet services, their tenure with the company, usage of additional services like online security, and their
chosen payment methods. The dataset is particularly useful for analyzing patterns and factors that contribute to customer churn, helping the company understand and potentially mitigate reasons for customer departure.'
